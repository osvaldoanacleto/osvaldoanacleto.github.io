Osvaldo Anacleto

I am a Lecturer in Statistics and Data Science at the Institute of Mathematical and Computer Sciences, University of São Paulo. I am also a researcher of the Center of Mathematical Sciences Applied to Industry (CeMEAI).

My research focuses on the development of novel statistical and machine learning methods for applications in biology, engineering and finance.
